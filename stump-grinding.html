A leftover stump is more than an eyesore. It's a tripping hazard, a mower-killer, and an open invitation to termites and carpenter ants near the house. We grind stumps several inches below grade so you can replant, lay sod, or simply reclaim the space.

We grind stumps of any size across the Lake Murray area, rake out the grindings, and leave a clean, fillable hole. Surface roots can be ground down too.

What's included

  • Grinding the stump 4–8 inches below grade for replanting or sod
  • Surface-root grinding where needed
  • Cleanup of grindings (left on site as mulch or hauled away)
  • Grinding of hard-to-reach and backyard stumps

Tree service FAQs: Stump Grinding & Removal

How much does stump grinding cost?

Single stumps in the Lake Murray area usually run about $100 to $400, with price driven mainly by the stump's diameter and root spread. Grinding several stumps in one visit lowers the cost per stump. Estimates are free.

What's the difference between stump grinding and stump removal?

Grinding chips the stump down several inches below grade with a machine, which is faster, cheaper, and less disruptive to your yard. Full removal pulls the entire root ball out, leaving a large hole. Grinding is the right choice for most homeowners.

Can I plant grass or a new tree where the stump was?

Yes. Once ground below grade and topped with soil, the spot is ready for sod or seed. If you want to plant a new tree in the exact spot, we can grind deeper and clear more root material for you.
